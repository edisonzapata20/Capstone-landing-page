Capstone Campus Life (Tentative Name)

Enpowering students to explore and join student organization in their university

Problem

Currently, there is no area where students can learn from all these teams and organizations in one single space, which makes it harder for the students to join them. The lack of exposure makes it harder for the organizations to recruit new qualified members. There is a need for a place where students can learn about organizations, explore their activities and see if they match their interests. At the same time, there is a need for a place where organizations can showcase their work, present their achievements and recruit new members easily.

Our solution to solve it?

Our solution is to create a web application that serves as a catalog for student organizations to present their work to students who are in search of a student organization to join and want to see the options available in a singular searching space. These organizations will help students explore different interests in their field of study and inspire them to pursue those interests in their future careers. Lastly, it's important to note that this web-application will not function as a Social Media platform but instead a listing where students and professors can list their organizations for general knowledge of the users utilizing the web-application as well as the features mentioned before.
